Our Mission

We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to removing plastic waste from the oceans and protecting marine ecosystems through technology, innovation, and community engagement.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Ocean Cleanup began with a single mission: to rid the world's oceans of plastic. Our team includes scientists, volunteers, and ocean lovers around the globe.
